# Christmas Season Tour with Don Meon
![image](/images/donmeon2.jpg)
Join Don Meon this Christmas season for several interesting tours. The tour is titled the season of hope.  Featuring special guest, Brooke Nicholls and presented by Unite Productions.
It will take place on different days from the 5th to 10th of December, 2025 by 7pm and is open for everyone. The tour will start with a world gospel Christmas festival in Ireland.
The first day of the tour will take place at One Church, 1000 Gorham St. 
The second day of the tour will be at Forward Church, 2885 Kennedy Rd. featuring The delectable Women of Faith. The third day of the tour will be held at Canada Event Centre, 2885 Kennedy Rd. featuring the amazing Hillsongs gospel group. The fourth day will be held at Trinity Pentecostal Church, 55 Franklin Blvd featuring some African gospel artists from Nigeria, Mercy Chinwo, Frank Edwards and Ada Ehi. The final day of the tour will be held at Dublin City Centre.
