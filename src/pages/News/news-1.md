# New Christmas Gospel Song Alert


![image](/images/donmeon1.jpg)

Sensational gospel artist- Don Meon has released a new Christmas song that has got everyone talking. The title of the song is Christmas Season of Love. An album collection of interesting Christmas songs that have never been heard. This offers a new colour and vibe to the 2025 Christmas season as the song came right on time in December.
The song has since been trending on all social media platforms and online music streaming platforms. Currently, the album ranks number one gospel song on Spotify and Apple Music with 98 million streams.
Don Meon has captured a lot of hearts and much more this season with his new song. In one of the tracks in the album, he featured American gospel singer, Travis Greene. Don Meon also perform his Christmas songs during the World Christmas Festival to be held in Ireland on the 5th of December, 2025 alongside several artists.
